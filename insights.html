Featured Governance April 2026

Does your Investment Policy Statement still reflect your charity?

An IPS is a legal requirement for any charity that has delegated discretionary investment management to an external manager. Yet the vast majority of charities that hold investments either do not have a current IPS, or are working from a document written years ago and never revisited.

The consequences of an out-of-date IPS are not merely administrative. A document that no longer reflects the charity's ethical position exposes trustees to reputational risk and creates gaps that the Charity Commission would expect a well-governed board to have addressed.
